About Teknikum

Teknikum is a leading upper secondary school in the areas of technology, people and society. We listen to the needs of the business community and adapt our education. It is your best guarantee for getting a job.

At Teknikum, students take a step into the future from the first day of school. We focus on technology as part of learning, and this can be expressed in everything from challenging experiments to realistic project work. With us, you will simply be well prepared for tomorrow's challenges.

On the way to the future, we give you a safe high school experience. Teknikum is a fantastic school, where we have fun together. Here there is an enriching diversity that allows everyone to be themselves. It gives us a nice atmosphere and cohesion.

As a student at Teknikum, you will meet committed, experienced and skilled teachers with cutting-edge expertise who care about you and want you to succeed. After your three years at our school, you will be ready to make a difference in society and become part of the solutions of the future.
